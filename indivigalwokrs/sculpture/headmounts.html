Head mounts

An older collection of taxidermized head mounts found and collected by our patron. All kept together due to their mass collection during the summer of 2013

  

Sulfur Goat

Mixed media

2013

This goat was the first specimen brought back from the inaugural journey to the planet of Lyp, while this species came back from the planet unarmed it is now known that this juvenile male was uncharacteristic of its species and is now considered an outlier.

 

Juvenile Greater Mountain Drake

Mixed media

2013

This winged specimen was a resident of the Collinn park zoo. It was one of the first successfully hatched captive eggs along with its siblings, though this young female did not make it though it’s first rough winter.

  

Western Worg

Mixed media

2013

Due to the controversial nature of this item, it is no longer shown in the museum.

 

Tapinoma sessile queen

Mixed media

2013

This specimen was one of many found in the woods outside of the patron’s house during the early days of the museum. the queen was found along with the others in the colony. After removal of the colony the queen was taxidermized and shown in the Museum along with information on how to deal with infestations safely.
