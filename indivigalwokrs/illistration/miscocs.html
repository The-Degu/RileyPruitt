Adeline Gauthier

When your family does not want you anymore, and you have powers, you do not know how to control where do you go? To the circus, of course! At least that is what Adeline tells herself, not the truth that she scared her parents so much that they gave her up the first moment they had the chance. Though that does not bother her so much anymore, she has a real family under the big top now, friends and freaks that know what it's like to be a little more than natural.

Now he has to find his way, and try to understand what the world is beyond the ring.
