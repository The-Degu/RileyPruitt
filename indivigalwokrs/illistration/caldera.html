The 12 races

In the world of Scodus the twelve races found there are the best of the best, or at least not linked to somewhere else. They exist in this world as a defiant strike against the powers working against them,  from the huge minotaur and warforged to the diminutive gnome and Kobold each race has it’s niche but is able to adapt beyond what their ancestors thought was possible

God symbols
